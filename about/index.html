About

Home Port is a blog about games and stories of any kind.

I'm MrCruesliPants, but I also go by MCP or Mac. You can find me on Twitter, Reddit and Steam. If you're interested, I keep track of the games I've been playing on HowLongToBeat, the movies I have seen on IMDb, the animes I'm watching on MyAnimeList, the books that I'm reading on Goodreads and the board games that I'm interested in on BoardGameGeek.

I have a YouTube and Twitch channel where I post videos.

I would love to hear from you!

This blog is hosted on GitHub pages using Jekyll and an adapted version of the Mickey theme developed by Vincent Chan.
